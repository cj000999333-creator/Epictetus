Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how epictetus.world ("we", "us", "the platform") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our website and services.

What we collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the platform:

  • Email address — if you voluntarily submit it via the waitlist form. We use this only to notify you of platform updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party.
  • Conversation content — messages you send to the AI are processed by Anthropic's API in order to generate responses. We do not store conversation history on our servers. Each session starts fresh.
  • Analytics data — we use GoatCounter, a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. It records page views and approximate country of origin only.

Cookies

We use a single cookie to remember that you have dismissed the cookie notice. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic API — your messages are processed by Anthropic to generate AI responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed through their API. See anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Google Fonts — we load fonts from Google's servers. Google may collect standard web server logs. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • GoatCounter — anonymous, cookie-free analytics. No personal data collected. See goatcounter.com/privacy.
  • Netlify — our hosting provider processes web traffic. Netlify's privacy policy applies. See netlify.com/privacy.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • The right to access data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

Data retention

Email addresses submitted via the waitlist are retained until you request deletion. Conversation data is not stored by us — it exists only in your browser session and is discarded when you close the page.

Children

This platform is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
